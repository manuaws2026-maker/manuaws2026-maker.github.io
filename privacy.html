Privacy Policy for IceCubes

Last Updated: December 8, 2024

Overview

IceCubes is a desktop application that helps you take AI-powered meeting notes. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

IceCubes collects and processes the following data locally on your device:

  • Audio Data: Meeting audio captured temporarily for transcription
  • Transcriptions: Text transcriptions of your meetings
  • Meeting Notes: AI-generated summaries and your notes
  • Calendar Data: Meeting info from Google Calendar (read-only)

Data Storage

  • All user data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is sent to our servers - we don't operate any servers
  • API calls go directly from your device to OpenAI, Deepgram, and Google

Google Calendar Integration

IceCubes requests read-only access to your Google Calendar to:

  • Display upcoming meetings
  • Auto-detect when meetings start
  • Show meeting attendees in notes

We do NOT modify your calendar, store data on servers, or share your information.

Data Security

  • Sensitive data (API keys, tokens) encrypted via macOS Keychain
  • No cloud storage - data never leaves your device
  • No analytics or telemetry collected
